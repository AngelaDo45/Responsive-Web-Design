What are Eco-Bricks?

  • Eco bricks are water bottles filled with single use plastics such as grocery bags, labels, and food packaging. Once the plastic bottles are filled, they become a hard, sturdy piece of equipment that can be used for building structures such as houses and furniture.

What are the Pros?

  • Removes plastic from oceans and landfills
  • Can be used for structrual support for shelter
  • Can help individuals gain context to the amount of single use plastic that is consumed and polluted.

What are the Cons?

  • Methane build up
  • Susceptible to heat, pressure, leeching
